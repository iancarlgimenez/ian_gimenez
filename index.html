About Me

Hi. I am Ian Carl Gimenez. I am a freelance full stack web developer and a graphic designer. I am also a self taught artist since young, even before I took up a formal design course. Growing up I learned how to embrace the technology as a medium and tool to express creativity.

From simply doing the traditional visual art such as drawing and painting, I learned graphic design and web design along the way until I got the hang of it and became one of my biggest passion.

Creative imagination, observation and research are my main instruments that help me to become successful in my previous works and projects. I love to get inspiration from what I see in my surrounding, as well as from other artists and designers.
